Admission Process & Eligibility

Steps to Apply

  1. Choose your preferred country and university.
  2. Submit your application with required documents.
  3. Receive an admission letter.
  4. Apply for a student visa.
  5. Travel and start your MBBS journey!

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in 12th grade (PCB).
  • NEET qualification (for Indian students).
  • Valid passport.
